Saturday Jazz

Saturday Jazz Matin√©es

On The Pilot's Main Floor every Saturday from 3:00-5:30. There is a $10 cover at the door and all seating is done on a first come, first seated basis. 

If you'd like to play at The Pilot please email jazz@thepilot.ca.

Photo: Alto-tude. Courtesy of Lloyd Smith. 

July Lineup

Saturday, July 6th - Alto-Tude - 3 Altos, No Waiting.  Jake Koffman, Alto Sax;  Bobby Brough, Alto Sax; Jesse Ryan, Alto Sax; Nathan Hiltz, Guitar; Lauren Falls, Bass; Ethan Ardelli, Drums. 

Saturday, July 13th - The Short Wayners Tribute to Herbie Hancock. Chris Gale, Sax; Matt Newton, Piano; Michael Herring, Bass; Tim Shea, Drums.

Saturday, July 20th - Norbert Botos Quartet. Norbert Botos, Drums; Mattlaus Gretzinger, Sax; Ewen Farncombe, Piano; Jonathan Chapman, Bass. 

Saturday, July 27th- From Vancouver Ryan Oliver Quintet. Ryan Oliver, Sax; Tim Hamel, Trumpet; Bernie Senensky, Piano; Pat Collins, Bass; Terry Clarke, Drums.
