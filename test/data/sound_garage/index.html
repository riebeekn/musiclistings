    ABOUT US

    Tucked into a gritty, century-old horse stable in Toronto’s Geary Avenue arts district, Blood Brothers brews beer that subverts expectations and rewrites the rules. With 26 taps of unapologetic flavour, Southern Comfort dishes that stick with you, and fridges stocked with ice cold cans and bottles to-go, we’re here to turn curiosity into conviction.
