Box Office

Online at ticketmaster.com

Venue box office will open one hour before door time. Tickets for the night of show will be available based on ticket availablity. You can also puchase tickets for other events at History at the venue box office.


Do I need to have the COVID-19 vaccine to attend a show?

Please visit our Health & Safety page for full details on this.

What are the set times for a specific show?

The door time will always be listed on your ticket/order, but we do not have full set times until the day of the show. Once finalized, set times can be found on our social channels:

  • Twitter - @HistoryToronto

  • Instagram - @historytoronto

  • Facebook - HistoryToronto

Where can I purchase tickets?

Tickets can be purchased online at www.ticketmaster.ca, or at the venue box office (subject to availability).

Where can I park?

Add parking to your ticket purchase for a guaranteed space in the parking lot nearest to the venue, conveniently located at 1167 Eastern Avenue (behind the venue, south of the Green P). If parking is still available when you complete your ticketing transaction, you will be prompted if you want to purchase a spot.

If you have already purchased your show tickets and would like to add parking, select the show from this link, and follow the prompts to complete the transaction. 

Parking is also available for purchase from the lot on the night of the show, based on availability. To avoid disappointment, purchase your parking tickets in advance.
