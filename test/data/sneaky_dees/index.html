frequently asked questions

How do I buy tickets for an event?

Tickets for most events are available online through our event listings. Some shows also have tickets at the door—get here early to snag one!

Can I book a private event at Sneaky Dee’s ?

Yes! We host everything from birthday bashes to full-on ragers. Hit up our contact page to ask about a private booking.

is there a dress code?

Nope. Come as you are—band tees, leather jackets, and ripped jeans welcome.

Do you have food available during events?

Absolutely! Our full menu is available during shows, so you can crush some nachos while catching a set.
